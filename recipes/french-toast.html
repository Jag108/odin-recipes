French Toast

Description

This simple French toast recipe soaks thick slices of bread in a rich egg and milk mixture to make golden, fluffy slices for a delicious breakfast treat. Works with white, whole wheat, brioche, cinnamon-raisin, Italian, or French bread! Delicious served hot with butter and maple syrup.

Ingredients:

  • ⅔ cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (Optional)
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon or nutmeg (Optional)
  • salt to taste
  • 6 thick slices bread
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, or more as needed

Steps:

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Step 2

    3. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl until combined and smooths.

  3. Step 3

    4. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat. Soak each slice of bread in egg mixture, about 5 to 10 seconds per side, allowing it to absorb the mixture without becoming soggy.

  4. Step 4

    5. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden brown and slightly crisp, 3 to 4 minutes per side.

  5. Step 5

    6. Serve hot and enjoy with butter and syrup.
